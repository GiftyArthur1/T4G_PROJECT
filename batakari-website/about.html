About Batakari Fugu

Rooted in tradition, crafted with pride — discover the story behind every thread.

Who We Are

Preserving Ghana's Iconic Fugu Tradition

Batakari Fugu is dedicated to celebrating and preserving Ghana's iconic Fugu tradition while making it accessible to the modern world. We collaborate directly with skilled northern artisans and bring vibrant, handcrafted garments to customers across Ghana and beyond.

Founded with a passion for cultural heritage, we believe that fashion can be a powerful bridge between generations — honoring the past while inspiring the future.

Our Mission & Values

Everything we do is guided by a commitment to culture, quality, and community.

Empower Artisans

We pay fair wages and provide sustainable livelihoods to skilled weavers and craftspeople in northern Ghana.

Promote Culture

We bring Ghanaian traditional fashion to a global audience, celebrating its beauty and significance.

Sustainability

We use natural dyes, eco-friendly packaging, and low-waste production methods to protect our environment.

Premium Quality

Every garment is inspected for quality before it reaches you — we never compromise on craftsmanship.

Our Journey

From a small workshop to a celebrated brand — here's how we grew.

2014

The Beginning

Founded in a small workshop in Tamale, with just three artisan partners and a dream to share Fugu with the world.

2017

Going Online

Launched our first online store, reaching customers across Ghana and the diaspora for the first time.

2020

Artisan Network

Expanded our artisan network to 30+ weavers, introducing new designs and custom order capabilities.

2024

500+ Customers

Celebrated serving over 500 happy customers and launched our premium couple's collection.
